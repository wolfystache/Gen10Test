The Silver Spoon:
Simple food, great ingredients. That's the name of the game here at The Silver Spoon. We know, that's becoming a cliche, but we truly take that mantra to heart. Our ethos isn't about re-inventing the wheel, but we do enjoy putting our own unique spin on classic American dishes, and bad puns of course. If you're not absolutely satisfied with one of our mouth-watering burgers, or our refreshing salads, it's on us. We know your life is complicated, but should your dining experience be? So if you're in the mood for fresh, delicious food at reasonable prices, in a warm and inviting atmosphere, come on down to The Silver Spoon. We hope you'll become one our regulars!
